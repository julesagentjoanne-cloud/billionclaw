# startupClaw - Full Report Template

## Purpose
Define the complete founder-facing report startupClaw should generate from the input and research.

## Simple overview
The report should feel like a useful startup intelligence document, not a vague diagnosis.

## Full report structure

### 1. Startup interpretation
- what you are likely building
- what job it likely does
- how startupClaw interprets the idea

### 2. Existing demand evidence
- what people are already doing today
- what workarounds / tools / spend prove demand exists
- what signals were found

### 3. Audience expansion
- original audience
- local optimum audience options
- global optimum audience options
- strongest audience candidate

### 4. Alternative buyer mapping
- original buyer
- alternative buyers
- sponsor / partner buyers
- strongest commercial buyer

### 5. Persona and urgency
- likely personas
- Cat persona
- Cheese persona(s)
- why one is stronger

### 6. Revenue proximity
- high / medium / low
- why
- GTM/pricing implication

### 7. Why now / founding insight / category story
- why now:
- founding insight:
- category story:

### 8. Distribution and trust
- distribution advantage:
- trust architecture needs:
- habit/workflow embedding path:

### 9. Domain specialist perspectives
- end-user view:
- manager view:
- executive view:
- alignment:
- conflict:
- strongest perspective for sale:

### 10. Competitor / alternative map
- direct competitors
- indirect alternatives
- do-nothing alternative
- likely wedge opportunity
- wedge-to-market expansion path:

### 10. Stage and bottleneck
- likely stage
- primary bottleneck
- secondary bottleneck
- confidence level

### 9. Best next move
- one recommendation
- top 3 actions
- success signal

### 10. Agent execution options
- which agents are most useful next
- what each one would produce
- which one is the fastest path to signal

### 11. What startupClaw did
- researched
- inferred
- used signals from
- still needs validation

### 12. Stress test summary (when useful)
- strongest parts of the startup:
- weakest parts of the startup:
- main constraint to becoming large:
- best next move:
